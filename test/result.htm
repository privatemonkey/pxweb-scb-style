Commodity group SITC

SITC (Standard International Trade Classification) is a UN recommended nomenclature of goods. It is hierarchical from 1-level digit to 5-level digit. SITC is based upon CN8-kodes. Since all CN8-codes do not use quantity other then metric tons (or it is accounted in different sorts, square meter, gram etc) this is not available in SITC. The current SITC (revision 4) is used since January 2007. SITCrev 3 is an older version.
